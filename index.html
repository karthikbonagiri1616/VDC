NSS Activities

Cleanliness Drive

Our NSS volunteers actively participate in Swachh Bharat campaigns, ensuring a clean and hygienic environment in campus and surrounding areas.

Tree Plantation

We organize tree plantation drives to spread awareness about environmental sustainability and contribute to greener surroundings.

Blood Donation Camp

NSS conducts regular blood donation camps, encouraging students to contribute towards saving lives and building a compassionate society.

Awareness Programs

Volunteers arrange health, civic, and social awareness programs in villages, creating positive impact in rural communities.

About Our College

Vaagdevi Junior & Degree College, established in 2005, is the first college in Boath constituency. We are committed to holistic education combining academics, sports, values, and discipline. Our mission is to prepare students for success in Defence, Engineering, Commerce, Arts, and various professional fields.

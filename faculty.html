Who We Are

Vaagdevi Defence Academy is among the top academies with expert faculty, study material, and personalized guidance to help students succeed.

Our History

2005 Established as the first Junior & Degree College in Boath constituency.
2005–2015 Expanded programs, producing graduates excelling in Defence, Engineering, Arts, and Commerce.
2015–Present Modernized with digital classes, strong infrastructure (5 acres campus), and Defence coaching.

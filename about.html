History

Vaagdevi Junior and Degree College was established in 2005. It is known for being the first college in Boath constituency. The college was created out of a need for higher education in Boath. High standard education was unique and important for rural people to make their desires happen, with accessibility and connection to the local culture.

The college has made significant contributions to academics and educational development of the region. Our students are settled in many professional fields like Engineering, Defence, Arts, Music, Commerce, Lecturing, and Teaching. Motivational and digital classes make students confident enough to crack any exam.

With 2 acres of ground and 3 acres of building, our infrastructure is one of our strengths. We have served many students in this region and now we are expanding to the Defence sector. We are confident that the coaching provided will give students a bright future.

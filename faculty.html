About Us

Empowering Students • Serving the Nation • Building Leaders

Who We Are

Vaagdevi Defence Academy is the top institute preparing future leaders for Defence & Professional careers. With expert faculty, curated study material, and personalized mentoring, we ensure student success.

We provide a learning environment where values meet academics, supported by modern facilities, sports, and co-curriculars. Our vision is to inspire confidence, discipline, and a passion for excellence in every student.

Our History

2005

Founded as the first Junior & Degree College in Boath constituency to provide higher education opportunities.

2005–2015

Expanded programs, producing graduates in Engineering, Defence, Arts, Commerce, and Education.

2015–Present

Introduced motivational & digital classes, built a 5-acre campus, and launched Defence coaching programs.

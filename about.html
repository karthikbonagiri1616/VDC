Our History

1995

College was established with a vision to provide quality education in Boath.

2005

Expanded with modern labs and digital classrooms.

2020

Became a leading institution in Adilabad District with over 2000 students.

Our journey began with a small group of dedicated educators who wanted to transform education in rural Telangana.

With commitment and hard work, we expanded facilities and introduced new courses.

We continue to focus on innovation, research, and holistic development of students.

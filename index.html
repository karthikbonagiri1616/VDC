About Us

Established in 2005, Vaagdevi Junior & Degree College, Boath, is the first higher education institution in the constituency. With expert faculty and modern infrastructure, we provide academic excellence blended with values, discipline, and defence training.

Our Defence Academy wing prepares aspirants with specialized coaching, physical training, and guidance for careers in the Armed Forces, Police, and other competitive sectors.
